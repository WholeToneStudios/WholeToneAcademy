About

Protest Choir Project is now active! All those who are Queer, Trans, and/or PoC: after your socially conscious Sunday afternoon activities or perhaps a much needed nap, come on over to the WholeTone Music Academy and magnify your voices! Alternatively, you can join us Tuesdays from 7-8:30pm and unwind after your work day. We'll continue learning how to be loud and proud without hurting ourselves in the process. You can look forward to some hands-on learning about body awareness at every session. Group voice lessons + slightly rowdy choir = Protest Choir Project. Pay-what-you-can $5-$25 but no one will be turned away for inability to pay. See you soon!

If you'd like to be on the Protest Choir Project mailing list, email us.

Polyphony, Harmonizing, & Belting on Sundays 5-6:30pm

Unison Singing & Chanting on Tuesdays 7-8:30pm

Located, as always, at the Department of Public Art, 312 Highland Ave, Somerville.

We are always working on making people of all types and stripes welcome, and consider ourselves an LGBTQA safe space.
